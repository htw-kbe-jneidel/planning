## Product Attributes

**product:**

- product:
  - id
  - name
  - image
  - category -> vat percentage
  - price brutto
- location
  - id
  - name
  - coords(lat,lon)

**storage:**

- quantities
  - location_id
  - product_id
  - amount of products
 
**vat-calculator:**

- vat cut of brutto price

**distance-calculator:**

- walking distance to other location
  - in minutes
  - in kilometers
